Craft Almanac is a material foraging map organized by season, habitat, and, uniquely, by the actual harvesting rule for the land under each point.

Its purpose is to empower makers of all kinds to build ethical and productive relationships with their local environments. Current maps include food, ink and dye, herbalism, and craft minerals, with aims to expand to include more three-dimensional craft materials.

Before you collect: occurrence is never permission. Confirm identification, property boundaries, posted rules, and current limits first. Some plants and fungi are toxic or have dangerous lookalikes; herbal content is educational only, not medical advice. Collect only where allowed, take lightly, and leave enough for growth and wildlife.
