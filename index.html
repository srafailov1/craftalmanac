Craft Almanac maps plants, fungi, and other local materials across the contiguous United States by season, habitat, and access.

Before collecting, confirm identification, property boundaries, posted rules, and current harvesting limits. Map points show observations, not permission to collect.

Some plants and fungi are toxic, have dangerous lookalikes, or require special preparation. Medicinal plant information is educational only and is not medical advice.

Please collect only where allowed, take lightly, and leave enough for continued growth and wildlife.
