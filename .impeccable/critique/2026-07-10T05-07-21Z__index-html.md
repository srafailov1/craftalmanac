## Overall Impression

The tool now reads as one continuous system from masthead to attribution line. The point card is the standout: ACCESS, then LAND naming the actual managing unit (resolved live from PAD-US), then the rule with its citation — the product's thesis argued in three rows, with nothing redundant left to bury it. The remaining gaps are small and mostly deliberate product questions rather than defects.

## What's Working

1. **The point card argues the thesis in three rows.** ACCESS → LAND ("Cass Park") → RULES with citation; the caution's "check who manages this land" now has its answer directly above it.
2. **One register family everywhere.** Zoom, geolocate, and offline controls wear the same panel/hairline/glow as the cards, in light and dark; the bottom edges of legend, season bar, and control stack sit on one 44px line.
3. **Errors are first-class citizens.** Search failures tell the truth, boot races heal themselves with a visible count line, and error statuses render in a legible warn tone at 12px.
4. **Accessibility depth held through the changes** — keyboard legend expansion animates to true content height, off-state chips stay readable, accessible names are clean.
